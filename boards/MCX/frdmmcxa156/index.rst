FRDM-MCXA156
####################

Overview
********

| The NXP FRDM-MCXA156 is a development board for the A15X/A14X 96 MHz Arm Cortex-M33 microcontroller.



.. image:: ./frdmmcxa156.png
   :width: 240px
   :align: center
   :alt: FRDM-MCXA156

 - Device: MCXA156
 - PartNumber: MCXA156VLL
