FRDM-MCXN947
####################

Overview
********

| The NXP FRDM-MCXN947 is a development board for the N94x 150 MHz Arm Cortex-M33 TrustZone microcontroller, which is for Industrial and Consumer IoT Applications.

 - Device: MCXN947
 - PartNumber: MCXN947VDF
