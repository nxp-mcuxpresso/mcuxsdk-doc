MCX-N9XX-EVK
####################

Overview
********

| The NXP N9xx-EVK is a development board for the N9xx 150 MHz Arm Dual Cortex-M33 TrustZone microcontroller, which is for Industrial and Consumer IoT Applications.

   :alt: MCX-N9XX-EVK

 - Device: MCXN947
 - PartNumber: MCXN947VDF
