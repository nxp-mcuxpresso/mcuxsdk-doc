FRDM-MCXW71
####################

Overview
********

The NXP FRDM-MCXW71 is a development board for the MCXW71 96 MHz Arm Cortex-M33 microcontroller.



.. image:: ./frdmmcxw71.png
   :width: 240px
   :align: center
   :alt: FRDM-MCXW71

MCU device and part on board is shown below:

 - Device: MCXW716C
 - PartNumber: MCXW716CMFTA
