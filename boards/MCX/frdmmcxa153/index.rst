FRDM-MCXA153
####################

Overview
********

| The NXP FRDM-MCXA153 is a development board for the A153 96 MHz Arm Cortex-M33 microcontroller.



.. image:: ./frdmmcxa153.png
   :width: 240px
   :align: center
   :alt: FRDM-MCXA153

MCU device and part on board is shown below:

 - Device: MCXA153
 - PartNumber: MCXA153VLH
