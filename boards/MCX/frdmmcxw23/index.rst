FRDM-MCXW23
####################

Overview
********

The NXP FRDM-MCXW23 is a development board for the MCXW23 32 MHz Arm Cortex-M33 microcontroller.



.. image:: ./frdmmcxw23.png
   :width: 1000px
   :align: center
   :alt: FRDM-MCXW23

MCU device and part on board is shown below:

 - Device: MCXW236B
 - PartNumber: MCXW236BIHNAR
