FRDM-RW612
####################

Overview
********

FRDM-RW612 is a compact and scalable development board for rapid prototyping of the RW61x series of Wi-Fi 6 + Bluetooth Low Energy + 802.15.4 tri-radio wireless MCUs. It offers easy access to the MCUâ€™s I/Os and peripherals, integrated open-standard serial interfaces, external flash memory and on-board MCU-Link debugger.
