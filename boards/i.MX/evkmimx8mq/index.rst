EVK-MIMX8MQ
####################

Overview
********

The i.MX 8MQuad family of boards provides a powerful and flexible development system for NXP's Cortex-M4 MCUs.

   :width: 240px
   :align: center
   :alt: EVK-MIMX8MQ

 - Device: MIMX8MQ6
 - PartNumber: MIMX8MQ6DVAJZ
