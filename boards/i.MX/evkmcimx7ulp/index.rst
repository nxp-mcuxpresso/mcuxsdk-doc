EVK-MCIMX7ULP
####################

Overview
********

The i.MX7ULP family of boards provides a powerful and flexible development system for NXP's Cortex-M4 MCUs.



.. image:: ./evkmcimx7ulp.png
   :width: 240px
   :align: center
   :alt: EVK-MCIMX7ULP

MCU device and part on board is shown below:

 - Device: MCIMX7U5
 - PartNumber: MCIMX7U5DVP07
