EVK-MIMXRT1064
####################

Overview
********

The NXP MIMXRT1064-EVK is a development board for the i.MX MIMXRT1064 600 MHz 32-bit ARM Cortex-M7 MCUs.



.. image:: ./evkmimxrt1064.png
   :width: 240px
   :align: center
   :alt: EVK-MIMXRT1064

MCU device and part on board is shown below:

 - Device: MIMXRT1064
 - PartNumber: MIMXRT1064DVL6A
